About us

Who we are

Voltex Tech is an EASA and UK CAA certified organization, specialized in aircraft maintenance services and airworthiness management for General Aviation.

Our mission is centered on safety and efficiency, providing top-quality services that ensure your aircraft meets all regulatory standards and operates at peak performance.

We are proud partners of leading aviation manufacturers including Diamond Aircraft, Continental Diesel Engines, and Tecnam Aircraft, holding official Service Center status.
