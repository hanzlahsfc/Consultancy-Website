About Us

We at Vortex Tech are a premier Combined Airworthiness Organization (CAO) specializing in Continuing Airworthiness Management (CAM) and Airworthiness Reviews (ARC) across Europe. Our organization is structured to deliver exceptionally agile, comprehensive digital compliance solutions.

We specialize in the end-to-end Airworthiness Management of light aircraft (MTOW under 2,730 kg), helicopters up to 1,200 kg, gliders, balloons, and airships under EASA Part-ML rules. Our mission is to translate high-level aviation safety theory into profitable, audit-proof operational reality.

By merging technical engineering legacy with advanced quality auditing and operational data, Vortex Tech delivers high-utility consulting, fleet management, and structural safety solutions. We operate with total geographic flexibility across Europe, seamlessly coordinating with your existing facilities or leveraging our deep network to help you source optimal hangar space whenever physical maintenance is required.

Backed by our professional membership in the German Aviation Research Society (GARS), we deliver high-level technical consulting and academic-grade corporate strategies that align your fleet management with cutting-edge aviation economic and safety research. Our team also holds active membership in the European Association of Aviation Psychology (EAAP), enabling us to deliver tailored human factors programs that enhance safety and minimize human error.
