About us

Who we are

VORTEX TECH is an aviation consulting and fleet services firm specializing in Light Aircraft (≤2730kg). We provide expert Airworthiness Compliance, Data Management, and Regulatory Consulting to ensure your aircraft operates safely and within all regulatory frameworks.

Our mission is centered on safety, efficiency, and regulatory precision, delivering top-quality consulting services that keep your fleet compliant with EASA and UK CAA standards.

We serve aircraft owners, operators, and fleet managers who need reliable, expert guidance across all aspects of aviation compliance and operations.
