# Vortex Tech — Combined Airworthiness Organization

A professional static website for Vortex Tech, a premier Combined Airworthiness Organization (CAO) specializing in Continuing Airworthiness Management (CAM), Airworthiness Reviews (ARC), and aviation compliance services across Europe.

## Features

- **Bilingual support** — Full English and Spanish versions
- **Responsive design** — Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Cinematic hero** — Full-viewport hero with gradient overlay and badge
- **Service showcase** — Grid-based service cards with hover effects
- **Fleet spotlight** — Dedicated section for aircraft available for dry lease
- **Human Factors training** — EASA Part-145/66 compliant training programs
- **Contact form** — Integrated inquiry system with map embed

## Local Development

Run a local server:

```bash
python -m http.server 8000
```

Then open `http://localhost:8000/` in your browser.

## About Vortex Tech

Vortex Tech is a premier Combined Airworthiness Organization (CAO) authorized under both EASA and UK CAA frameworks. We specialize in end-to-end Continuing Airworthiness Management (CAM) and Airworthiness Review Certificate (ARC) issuance for light aircraft, helicopters, gliders, balloons, and airships under EASA Part-ML rules.

Our mission is to translate high-level aviation safety theory into profitable, audit-proof operational reality. Backed by professional membership in the German Aviation Research Society (GARS) and the European Association of Aviation Psychology (EAAP), we deliver high-utility consulting, fleet management, and human factors training across Europe.
