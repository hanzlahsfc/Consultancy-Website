About Us

We at Vortex Tech are a premier Combined Airworthiness Organization (CAO) specializing in Continuing Airworthiness Management (CAM) and Airworthiness Reviews (ARC) across Europe. Our organization is structured to deliver exceptionally agile, comprehensive digital compliance solutions.

We specialize in the end-to-end Airworthiness Management of light aircraft (MTOW under 2,730 kg), helicopters up to 1,200 kg, gliders, balloons, and airships under EASA Part-ML rules. Our mission is to translate high-level aviation safety theory into profitable, audit-proof operational reality.

By merging technical engineering legacy with advanced quality auditing and operational data, Vortex Tech delivers high-utility consulting, fleet management, and structural safety solutions. We operate with total geographic flexibility across Europe, seamlessly coordinating with your existing facilities or leveraging our deep network to help you source optimal hangar space whenever physical maintenance is required.

Backed by our professional membership in the German Aviation Research Society (GARS), we deliver high-level technical consulting and academic-grade corporate strategies that align your fleet management with cutting-edge aviation economic and safety research. Our team also holds active membership in the European Association of Aviation Psychology (EAAP), enabling us to deliver tailored human factors programs that enhance safety and minimize human error.

Our Services

We provide a full range of aviation services to keep your aircraft safe, compliant, and operational.

Continuing Airworthiness Management (CAM)

We provide comprehensive airworthiness management explicitly tailored to light aircraft, helicopters, gliders, balloons, and airships under our specialized CAO scope. We oversee your regular maintenance programs, manage life-limited parts, track Airworthiness Directives (ADs), and control deferred defects to keep your fleet continuously compliant with all EASA regulatory frameworks.

ARC Issuance and Renewal

We officially handle your Airworthiness Review Certificate (ARC) processes. Our qualified team performs thorough records reviews and physical aircraft inspections to issue or extend EASA Form 15c, ensuring your fleet remains certified and legally compliant without administrative friction.

Pre-AESA Regulatory and ISO Auditing

We perform detailed, preventive airworthiness auditing to guarantee complete compliance. Merging EASA safety principles with rigorous ISO Lead Auditor frameworks, our evaluation processes identify latent risks and verify that your internal quality tracking meets international aviation standards, acting as a vital pre-assessment mock audit before authority inspections arrive.

Pre-Purchase Inspection

We provide comprehensive pre-purchase inspections to give you complete peace of mind before buying an aircraft. Our thorough evaluations analyze complete tech logs, structural history, and system configurations so you know the exact technical and regulatory status of your investment.

Aircraft Leasing

We provide tailored dry leasing options designed to match your operational requirements. We work closely with flight schools, aero clubs, and individual aviators to deliver flexible solutions that fit your specific timeline and budget.

Hour Building

Specialized, cost-effective options for training pilots who need to accumulate flight hours efficiently. Our Cessna 150 is ideal for hour building with fuel burn as low as 4 gallons per hour, keeping operating costs minimal. Flexible block hour packages and dry lease rates available, allowing you to build cross-country experience at your own pace.
